Hi! I’m Lesley. With Alegria started as a way of documenting my story as a millennial Latina deeply committed to family, culture, art, and community. Alegria, meaning joy in Spanish, is my last name and it’s with much alegria that I welcome you to my journey as a twenty something navigating life as a millennial Latina. My family’s Peruvian culture and my San Francisco upbringing inspire my food, style, and weekend adventures! As a result, I share my mama’s recipes influenced by Peruvian cuisine, creative DIY projects inspired by my papa’s craftsmanship, and weekend trips to my favorite Bay Area spots. You can follow more of my story on Instagram, Snapchat, and Twitter!
